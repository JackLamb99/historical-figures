How to Play

  • A card will present a historical figure, showcasing their name, a brief description, and their year of birth.

  • A second card will be displayed with only the name of another historical figure. Players will have 10 seconds to determine whether this individual was born before ('Older') or born after ('Younger') the first figure presented.

  • Each correct guess earns the player 1 point, the game will continue until the player makes an incorrect guess.
